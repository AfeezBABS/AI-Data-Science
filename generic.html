About Me

I am a passionate, results-driven Data Scientist with a strong foundation in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making. Over the years, I have honed my ability to transform raw data into actionable insights that drive business growth and operational efficiency. I firmly believe in the power of teamwork, collaboration, and the continuous pursuit of knowledge to create meaningful impact. My work ethos is built on professionalism, trust, and a genuine love for solving complex problems through data.

I currently hold three academic degrees, including a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from the University of Hull, UK (2024), a Master’s degree in Statistics (Biometry) from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria (2018), and a Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from the University of Ilorin, Nigeria (2014). My academic background, coupled with over seven years of professional experience in data science, has enabled me to excel in both technical and business environments.

Throughout my career, I have worked in various roles, gaining expertise in data analytics, statistical modeling, business intelligence, and predictive analytics. As a Data Analyst at Wema Bank, Nigeria, I played a critical role in evaluating and interpreting data to support business decisions, develop dashboards, and optimize customer segmentation. Additionally, my experience as a freelance Data Analyst on Fiverr and Upwork has allowed me to work with a diverse range of global clients, providing services in data visualization, predictive analytics, and research analysis across multiple industries, including healthcare, finance, and e-commerce.

Technical Proficiency

Statistical Analysis & Machine Learning – Python, R, SPSS

Data Visualization & Business Intelligence – Tableau, Power BI, Excel Dashboards

Database Management – SQL, relational databases, cloud-based storage solutions

Qualitative & Quantitative Data Analysis – NVivo, MAXQDA, ATLAS.ti

Automation & Macros – Advanced Excel Macros for process optimization

Open to Opportunities

Having recently completed my Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, I am actively seeking employment and freelance opportunities where I can apply my skills to solve complex data challenges and drive innovation. My goal is to continue making a meaningful impact through data-driven insights that enhance industries and improve lives.
